                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  $ \begin{aligned} \text{Total amount} &= \pounds 4 \\ &= 4 \times 100 \text{p} \\ &= 400 \text{p} \end{aligned} $

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Rodger pays back $30 \%$. So,

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  $ \begin{aligned} \text{Amount paid} &= 30\% \text{ of } 400 \\ &= \dfrac {30} {100} \times 400 \\ &= \dfrac {30 \times 400} {100} \\ &= \dfrac {30 \times 4 \cancel{0} \cancel{0}} {1 \cancel{0} \cancel{0}} \\ &= 30 \times 4 \\ &= 120 \text{p} \end{aligned} $

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  $ \begin{aligned} \text{Amount owed} &= 400 - 120 \\ &= 280 \text{p} \\ &= \pounds 2.80 \end{aligned} $

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  $ \begin{aligned} \text{Amount owed} &= 70\% \text{ of } 400 \\ &= \dfrac {70} {100} \times 400 \\ &= \dfrac {70 \times 400} {100} \\ &= \dfrac {70 \times 4 \cancel{0} \cancel{0}} {1 \cancel{0} \cancel{0}} \\ &= 70 \times 4 \\ &= 280 \\ &= 280 \text{p} \\ &= \pounds 2.80 \end{aligned} $
