Calculate the area of the triangle.

</div>
<div class='workings'>
<div class='working'>

Area of triangle is given by

$
\begin{aligned}
&= \dfrac {1} {2} \times a \times b
\end{aligned}
$

$
\begin{aligned}
&= \dfrac {1} {2} \times 3 \times 5 \\\\
&= \dfrac {15} {2} \\\\
&= 14.5 \text { cm}^2
\end{aligned}
