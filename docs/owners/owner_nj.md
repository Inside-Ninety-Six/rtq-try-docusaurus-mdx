    &= 5
\end{aligned}

The letter is $\text {E}$

Emily is one quarter the age of her Aunt Ingrid.

</div>

<div class='working'>

Let the age of Emily $= a$

$
\begin{aligned}
a + 4a &= 60 \\\\
