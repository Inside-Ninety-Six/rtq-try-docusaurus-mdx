              • $50\%$ of $10$
              • $40\%$ of $20$
              • $30\%$ of $30$

              $ \begin{aligned} &= 50\% \text{ of } 10 \\ &= \dfrac{50}{100} \times 10 \\ &= \dfrac{50 \times 10}{100} \\ &= \dfrac{5\cancel{0}\times 1 \cancel{0}}{1\cancel{0} \cancel{0}} \\ &= 5 \end{aligned} $

              Let's calculate the second value.

              $ \begin{aligned} &= 40\% \text{ of } \ 20 \\ &= \dfrac{40}{100} \times 20 \\ &= \dfrac{40 \times 20}{100} \\ &= \dfrac{4\cancel{0} \times 2\cancel{0}}{1\cancel{0}\cancel{0}} \\ &= 8 \end{aligned} $

              $ \begin{aligned} &= 30\% \text{ of } \ 30 \\ &= \dfrac{30}{100} \times 30 \\ &= \dfrac{30 \times 30}{100} \\ &= \dfrac{3\cancel{0} \times 3\cancel{0}}{1\cancel{0}\cancel{0}} \\ &= 9 \end{aligned} $

              Using the calculation above,
