<div class='question subquestion'>

In a ‘third off’ sale a bike is sold for $\pounds 114$.
How much would it have sold for before the sale?

</div>
<div class='workings'>
<div class='working'>

Let the price $= a$

As per the question,

$
\begin{aligned}
\dfrac {1} {3} \ \text{of} \ a      &= 114 \\\\
\dfrac {1} {3} \times a             &= 114 \\\\
a                                   &= 114 \times 3 \\\\
a                                   &= 342
\end{aligned}
$

</div>
</div>
<div class='answers'>
<div class='answer'>

$342$

</div>
