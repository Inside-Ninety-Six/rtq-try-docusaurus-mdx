                                                                                                      Homer, Marge and Bart each have $$1000$.

                                                                                                      Homer gives $10\%$ of his money to Marge. Marge then gives $10\%$ of her new amount to Bart. Bart then gives $10\%$ of his new amount to Homer.

                                                                                                      How much has Homer got now?

                                                                                                      $ \begin{aligned} \text{Amount Homer gives to Marge} &= 10 \% \ \text{of} \ 1000 \\ &= \dfrac {10} {100} \times 1000 \\ &= \dfrac {10 \times 1000} {100} \\ &= \dfrac {10 \times 10 \cancel 0 \cancel 0} {1 \cancel 0 \cancel 0} \\ &= 10 \times 10 \\ &= 100 \\ \text{New amount that Marge has} &= 1000 + 100 \\
                                                                                                      &= 1100 \\
                                                                                                      \text{Amount Marge gives to Bart} &= 10 \% \ \text{of} \ 1100 \\ &= \dfrac {10} {100} \times 1100 \\ &= \dfrac {10 \times 1100} {100} \\ &= \dfrac {10 \times 11 \cancel 0 \cancel 0} {1 \cancel 0 \cancel 0} \\ &= 10 \times 11 \\ &= 110 \\ \text{New amount that Bart has} &= 1000 + 110 \\
                                                                                                      &= 1110 \\
                                                                                                      \text{Amount Bart gives to Homer} &= 10 \% \ \text{of} \ 1110 \\ &= \dfrac {10} {100} \times 1110 \\ &= \dfrac {10 \times 1110} {100} \\ &= \dfrac {1 \cancel 0 \times 111 \cancel 0} {1 \cancel 0 \cancel 0} \\ &= 1 \times 111 \\ &= 111 \\ \text{New amount that Homer has} &= 900 + 111 \\
                                                                                                      &= 1011 \end{aligned} $

                                                                                                      $1011$
