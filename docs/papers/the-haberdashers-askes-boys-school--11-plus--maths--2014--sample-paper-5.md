<div class='question question'>

Children are offered a $25\%$ discount on the cost of an adult ticket to visit Hamshaw House. 
Senior citizens are given a $20\%$ discount. If a child's ticket costs $\pounds 30$, how much does a senior citizen pay?

</div>
<div class='workings'>
<div class='working'>

Let the cost of adult ticket $= a$

As per the question

$
\begin{aligned}
25\% \text{ of} a                               &= 30 \\\\
\dfrac {25a} {100}                              &= 30 \\\\
\dfrac {25a} {25 \times 4}                      &= 30 \\\\
\dfrac {\cancel {25} a} {\cancel {25} \times 4} &= 30 \\\\
\dfrac {a} {4}                                  &= 30  \\\\
a                                               &= \pounds 120
\end{aligned}
$

$
\begin{aligned}
\text{senior pay}  &= 20\% \text{ of} 120 \\\\
                   &= \dfrac {20} {100} \times 120 \\\\
                   &= \dfrac {2 \cancel {0} } {1\cancel {0} \cancel {0} } \times 12\cancel {0} \\\\
                   &= \pounds 24
                   
\end{aligned}
$

</div>
</div>
<div class='answers'>
<div class='answer'>

$\pounds 24$
