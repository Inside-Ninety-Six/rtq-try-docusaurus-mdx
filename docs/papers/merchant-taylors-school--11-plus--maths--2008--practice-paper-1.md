<div class='question question'>

I think of a number and multiply it by $3$ and then subtract $6$. If the result is $15$ what number was I thinking of?

</div>
<div class='workings'>
<div class='working'>

Let the number $= a$

As per the question,

$
\begin{aligned}
a \times 3 - 6   &= 15 \\\\
3 a - 6          &= 15 \\\\
3 a              &= 15 + 6 \\\\
3 a              &= 21 \\\\
a                &= \dfrac {21} {3} \\\\
a                &= \dfrac {3 \times 7} {3} \\\\
a                &= \dfrac {\cancel {3} \times 7} {\cancel {3}} \\\\
a                &= 7
\end{aligned}
$

</div>
</div>
<div class='answers'>
<div class='answer'>

$7$

</div>
</div>
