<div class='question question'>

What is the mass of $16\, tins$, each of mass $178\,g$?

Give your answer in **kilograms.**

</div>
<div class='workings'>
<div class='working'>

$
\begin{aligned}

\text{Mass of 16 tins}                  &= 16 \times 178 \text{ g} \\\\
                                        &= 2848 \text { g} \\\\
                                        &= \dfrac {2848} {1000} \text { kg} \\\\
                                        &= 2.848 \text { kg}
\end{aligned}
$

</div>

$2.848 \text { kg}$
