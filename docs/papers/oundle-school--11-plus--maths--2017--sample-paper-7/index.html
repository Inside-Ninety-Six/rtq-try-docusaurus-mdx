                                        $ \begin{aligned} x \times x + 2 \times x + 4 &= 9177 \\ x &= \sqrt[3]{9177} \\ x &= 21 \text{approx.} \\ \text{So the numbers are} &= 19 , 21 , 23 \\ \text{Sum of these numbers are} &= 19 + 21 + 23 \\ &= 63 \end{aligned} $

                                        $63$
