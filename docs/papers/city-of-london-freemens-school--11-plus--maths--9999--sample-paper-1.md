Also the sum of angles in a triangle is $180 \degree$. So,

$
\begin{aligned}
b + 69                                                  &= 132 \\\\
b                                                       &= 132 - 69 \\\\
b                                                       &= 63 \\\\
a + b + 69                                              &= 180 \\\\
a + 63 + 69                                             &= 180 \\\\
a + 132                                                 &= 180 \\\\
a                                                       &= 180 - 132 \\\\
a                                                       &= 48
\end{aligned}
$
