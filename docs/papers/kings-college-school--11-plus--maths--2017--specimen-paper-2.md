Calculate how many seconds there are in $15 \dfrac{1}{2}$ hours.

&= 15 \dfrac {1} {2} \\\\
&= 15 \times 2 {}+ \dfrac {1} {2} \\\\
&= \dfrac  {30 {}+ 1} {2} \\\\
&= \dfrac {31} {2} \\\\
&= 15.5 \\\\
&= \text {1 hour has 3600 seconds} \\\\
&= 15.5 \times 3600 \\\\
&= 55,800 \text {seconds}
\end {aligned}
$

</div>
