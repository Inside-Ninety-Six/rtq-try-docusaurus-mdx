\begin{aligned}

a                   &= \dfrac {36} {4} \\\\
a                   &= \dfrac {9 \times 4} {4} \\\\
a                   &= \dfrac {9 \times \cancel {4} } {\cancel{4}} \\\\
a                   &= 9
\end{aligned}
$

</div>
</div>

$9$

                                   &= 90 \times 0.45 \\\\
                                   &= 40.5 \ \text {g} \\\\

                                   &= 122 \ \text {g}
