<div class='question question'>

I am thinking of two numbers. 

If I double the first and add the second, my answer is $35$. 

If I double the second and subtract the first, my answer is $15$.

By forming and solving two simultaneous equations, find the two numbers.

</div>

<div class='working'>

Let the first number $= a$

the second number $= b$

As per the question

$
\begin{aligned}
2a + b &= 35 \\\\
2b - a &= 15 \\\\
\end{aligned}
$

First let's find a

$
\begin{aligned}
2b - a          &= 15 \\\\
2(35 - 2a) - a  &= 15 \\\\
70 - 14a - a    &= 15 \\\\
70 - 15a        &= 15 \\\\
-15a            &= 15 - 70 \\\\
-15a            &= -65 \\\\
a               &= \dfrac { \cancel{-} 65 }{ \cancel{-} 15 } \\\\
a               &= \dfrac {65} {15} \\\\
a               &= 4.3
\end{aligned}
$

$
\begin{aligned}
2a + b           &= 35 \\\\
2 \times 4.3 + b &= 35 \\\\
8.3          + b &= 35 \\\\
b                &= 35 - 
\end{aligned}
$
